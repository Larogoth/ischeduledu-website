Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about iSchedulEDU app features, technical support, emergency scenarios, and advanced features. Learn how to create fire drill schedules, handle weather delays, manage assembly schedules, and use QR code sharing.

How does iSchedulEDU handle emergency scheduling?

iSchedulEDU uses intelligent algorithms to automatically redistribute remaining time into equal class periods when disruptions occur. Teachers input their start time, end time, and number of class rotations, and the app generates a balanced schedule in under 2 minutes.

What types of emergency scenarios does iSchedulEDU support?

The app handles fire drills, weather delays, assemblies, substitute teacher scenarios, early dismissals, and any schedule disruption that requires quick equal-time redistribution.

How does the equal time distribution work?

Our proprietary algorithms divide remaining time into equal periods. When perfect division isn't possible, the app makes strategic decisions to ensure maximum fairness across all subjects.

Can I share schedules with other teachers?

Yes, iSchedulEDU offers multiple sharing options: QR codes for instant scanning, universal links for app users and non-app users, text message sharing, and PDF exports.

What devices are supported?

iSchedulEDU requires iOS 17.6 or later and iPadOS 17.6 or later. The app is optimized for both iPhone and iPad devices.

Is the app free to use?

iSchedulEDU offers 4 free schedule generation sessions to get started. Additional premium features are available through in-app purchases.

How do notifications work?

Teachers can set up daily notifications for schedule changes, configure notification times, customize messages, and ensure all stakeholders are informed of schedule modifications.

Can I create custom schedules?

Yes, iSchedulEDU excels at creating custom daily schedules with specific times, periods, activities, and personalized schedules for regular days, special events, and unique school situations.
