How does iSchedulEDU work? We use intelligent algorithms to automatically redistribute remaining time into equal class periods when disruptions occur. Teachers simply input their start time, end time, and number of class rotations, and our app generates a balanced schedule in under 2 minutes. It's that simple!

What emergency scenarios can iSchedulEDU handle? Our app handles fire drills, weather delays, assemblies, substitute teacher scenarios, early dismissals, and any schedule disruption that requires quick equal-time redistribution. Whatever emergency comes up, we've got you covered!

How does the equal time distribution work? Our proprietary algorithms divide remaining time into equal periods. When perfect division isn't possible, the app makes strategic decisions to ensure maximum fairness across all subjects. Every class gets the time they deserve!

Can I share schedules with other teachers? Absolutely! iSchedulEDU offers multiple sharing options: QR codes for instant scanning, universal links for app users and non-app users, text message sharing, and PDF exports. We make communication easy!

What devices are supported? iSchedulEDU requires iOS 17.6 or later and iPadOS 17.6 or later. The app is optimized for both iPhone and iPad devices, so you can use it on whatever device you prefer!

Is iSchedulEDU really free? Yes! We offer 4 free schedule generation sessions to get you started. Additional premium features are available through in-app purchases, but you can try everything out first!

How do notifications work? Teachers can set up daily notifications for schedule changes, configure notification times, customize messages, and ensure all stakeholders are informed of schedule modifications. We make sure everyone stays in the loop!

Can I create custom daily schedules? Absolutely! iSchedulEDU excels at creating custom daily schedules with specific times, periods, activities, and personalized schedules for regular days, special events, and unique school situations. We give you complete control!
